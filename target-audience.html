Target Audience and Scenarios

The site's Target Audience will be College students, male and female, attending any given campus.

  • Age: Between 18-25 years old
  • Experience: Students
  • Income: N/A
  • Motivation: As the purpose stated, the motivation is to become a site that can be daily linked with thr students life (mostly activities and newspaper), so they can have all the necesary information to plan in advance their upcomming calendar.
  • How: Mostly intended to be mobile friendly, since students schedule their activities on their phone calendar. Not limited to mobile, but intended to being accesible on any PC, laptop.
